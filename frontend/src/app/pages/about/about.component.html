About TF2SC

Create and share TF2 subclasses

Since the dawn of the gold rush update back in 2008, people started experimenting with different weapons and experienced a shift in playstyle. With time, this phenomenon was named "subclasses", which is a Mercenary (also named class) together with special weapons and a distinct playstyle. TF2SC aims to be a quick and easy way of categorizing different subclasses, creating and sharing them.
